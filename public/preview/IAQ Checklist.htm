1. Has there been damage to the home from water entering the basement, living area or ceilings? Yes No

2. Do you have water leakage in the basement of the building? Yes No

4. Does the home have a forced air heating & cooling system and dusting is needed frequently? Yes No

6. Do you see discoloration or mold on the bathroom or shower walls? Yes No

11. Do you feel better when you are outside your home or workplace? Yes No
