Whole House, HEPA / Carbon, UV Air Filtration Systems

Pollution Research, experts in solving indoor air quality (IAQ) problems introduces a new line of whole house, HEPA / Carbon, UV Air Filtration Systems, primarily for home applications. In addition, Pollution Research has developed a self-standing independent air purification systems for commercial and industrial applications.

The ultraviolet (UV) component of sunlight is the main reason microbes die in outdoor air. The use of ultraviolet germicidal light for sterilization of micro-organisms has been studied since the 1930's. Microbes are vulnerable to the effects of light at the wavelengths at or near 253.7 nm. At that wavelength ultraviolet light has the right amount of energy to break bonds or genetically damage microorganisms. Ultraviolet systems have much more concentrated levels of ultraviolet energy than are found in sunlight.

Mold contamination can actually be found lining your heating and air conditioning systems duct work. Ultra violet germicidal (UVC) light can help remove airborne populations of mold, bacteria and viruses in your home. Properly designed UV system installations have proven highly effective in homes, hospitals, schools, etc.

For: Pre-existing and New Construction

1. When using an HVAC system... you will be bringing in air from the outside as well as recycled inside air and running it thru a HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Arrestor) Filter, plus carbon before the HVAC System. As a result, you are now ridding all contamination being introduced to the motor, coils, heating elements and ultimately all ductwork, delivering healthy, highly filtered clean air to the room.

2. When using radiant heat...in order to purify the air, it is necessary to use a heat exchange unit in conjunction with our MOD 10 or MOD 10 UV air filtration system.

3. Our HEPA/Carbon Filtration System with UV Germicidal Lighting, adds a new dimension which is a process of disinfecting mold, bacteria and infectious diseases and decreasing volatile organic compounds in the air stream. UV lighting is becoming very popular with the home owner today.
