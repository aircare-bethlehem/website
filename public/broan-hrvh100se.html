The BROAN HRVH100SE Heat Recovery Ventilator with HEPA Filtration

Model HRVH100SE
Price: Call First!

The HRVH100SE Heat Recovery Ventilator is equipped with a HEPA filter that removes 99.97% of allergens and other microscopic particles in the air, significantly increasing the quality of air inside your home. It is an obvious energy-efficient choice with its ENERGY STAR® certification. The BROAN™ HRVH100SE has a cutting edge blower design, allowing for an ultra-quiet operation which is barely audible at low speed (2.5 sones), equivalent to the sound of a standard refrigerator.


HEPA Filtration plus Balanced Ventilation Systems provide year-round air filtration, plus energy saving features matched to any climate.

BROAN’s air exchangers accomplish a "balanced" volume exchange of indoor and outdoor air. This balance ensures that unwanted air is not pulled into wall cavities and voids in the home, which can cause mold and mildew issues. Additionally, the exchange of air does not affect the operation of naturally vented combustion appliances.

  • Delivers 50 to 104 CFM of balanced ventilation
  • 5-inch diameter ports use smaller ducts allowing for easy installation
  • Integrated defrost control allows for a fast and easy adjustment of the defrost duration according to climatic zones
  • Electronic wall control with LCD screen included offers 5 operation modes (Auto, Economic, Continuous, Re-circulate, and Turbo) for optimal performance
  • Removable terminal block to ease wall control connections
  • Blower design gives a ultra-quiet operation at 2.5 sones
  • HVI certified and ENERGY STAR® Qualified*


HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) with antimicrobial protection is the most effective filtration available, capturing 99.97% of airborne particles including dust, allergens and pet dander.

Continuous HEPA filtration provides year round reduction of particles in the air of your home, while reducing dust build up on surfaces.
