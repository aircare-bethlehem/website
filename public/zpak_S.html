Z-Pak Rigid Cell Filters

The Glasfloss Z-Pak extended surface filter is designed for medium and high efficiency applications and used in a range of commercial and industrial applications. Z-Pak filters are offered in four efficiency performance levels: 40-45%, 60-65%, 80-85% and 90-95%.

Z-Pak filters are available in a variety of filter face dimensions with two optional depths. Z-Pak filters can be interchanged or adapted to any existing filter systems.

  • High Efficiency Synthetic or Fiberglass Media Available
  • Injection Molded Pleat Separators
  • Available in Box or Header Styles
  • 6" and 12" Depths
  • High Dust Holding Capacity
  • Ideal for VAV Systems
