~ Do it Yourself Indoor Air Quality Checklist ~


Indoor Air Quality Home Checklist
  1. Do you need to dust frequently or notice particles in the air in bright sunlight from doors and windows? Yes No

  2. Does the home have a forced air heating & cooling system and dusting is needed frequently? Yes No

  3. Are you using only a fiberglass filter in your forced air heating system?
    Yes No

  4. Do you see water stains on your basement walls, ceiling joists or floors?
    Yes No

  5. Do you notice a musty or stale odor from the basement but not elsewhere in your home? Yes No

  6. Does anyone in your family experience discomfort such as itchy eyes, sore throat or headaches that go away when out of the home?
    Yes No

  7. Do you have a fireplace that smells or does not have a chimney cap?
    Yes No

  8. Do your carpeted rooms show darker edges along the walls or underneath the doors? Yes No

  9. Do you notice that cooking odors linger in your home even after several hours? Yes No

  10. Do you have pets such as cats, dogs, gerbils, hamsters or birds in your home? Yes No

  11. Do you have exposed fiberglass insulation in your basement or storage areas? Yes No

  12. Do you see dark areas on the wood in your attic or unfinished basement?
    Yes No

  13. Do you have a heating or AC unit in an unfinished attic space?
    Yes No
